Lasagna

Description

Layers of soft oozy pasta sheets sandwiched between fragrant tomatoey mince. A dish that's irresistable!

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Mince of choice
  • Passata
  • Cheese of choice

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven
  2. Soak pasta sheets
  3. Make the mince
  4. Add passata

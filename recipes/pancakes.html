Pancakes

Description

Soft, fluffy pancakes that's delicious with fresh fruit and a drizzle of honey or maple syrup. A definite crowd pleaser!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk of choice
  • Eggs (optional)
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Make the batter
  2. heat pan with oil
  3. Pour batter in the pan
  4. Flip when top is bubbling
